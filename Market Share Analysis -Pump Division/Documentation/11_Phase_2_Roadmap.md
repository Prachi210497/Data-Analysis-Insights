# 11_Phase_2_Roadmap.md

## Status
Phase 2 is **planned and defined at a conceptual level**.  
Implementation will be taken up based on business priorities, stakeholder alignment, and feasibility.

---

## Objective of Phase 2

The objective of Phase 2 is to evolve the Market Share Analysis solution from **descriptive reporting** to **proactive and predictive decision support**, enabling stronger business growth and scalability.

---

## Planned Enhancement Areas

### 1. Data Automation & Refresh
- Automate data ingestion from source systems
- Reduce manual dependencies
- Enable scheduled refresh with monitoring

**Expected Benefit:**  
Improved data freshness and operational efficiency.

---

### 2. Conversion & Performance Analytics
- Track conversion from Installed Base to Order Booking
- Introduce service-person and region-level conversion KPIs
- Add rolling trend analysis (3M / 6M / 12M)

**Expected Benefit:**  
Better visibility into performance gaps and drivers.

---

### 3. Predictive & Risk Indicators
- Identify pumps at risk of moving to standby or lost status
- Highlight customers with declining order behavior
- Introduce early warning signals based on historical trends

**Expected Benefit:**  
Proactive intervention and improved retention.

---

### 4. Customer & Account Intelligence
- Add customer-level health indicators
- Enable drill-through views for key accounts
- Support targeted engagement strategies

**Expected Benefit:**  
Improved account prioritization and revenue realization.

---

### 5. Executive & Leadership Views
- Create executive-focused dashboards
- Highlight top risks, opportunities, and trends
- Enable faster decision-making for leadership reviews

**Expected Benefit:**  
Clear, decision-ready insights for senior stakeholders.

---

### 6. Security & Governance Enhancements
- Extend Row-Level Security to additional hierarchies
- Strengthen access control and auditability
- Maintain centralized security design

**Expected Benefit:**  
Scalable and compliant enterprise reporting.
