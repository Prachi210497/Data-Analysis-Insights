# 09_Training_Support

## 1. Objective

In this phase **enabled business users to effectively understand, use, and adopt** the Market Share Analysis dashboards.

Training and support ensured that stakeholders could **interpret insights correctly**, use reports independently, and rely on the dashboards for regular decision-making.

---

## 2. Training Approach

Training was conducted in a **structured and role-based manner** to address different user needs.

### Key training methods included:

* Live walkthrough sessions of dashboards
* Explanation of KPIs and business definitions
* Demonstration of filters, slicers, and drill-down features
* Use-case-based examples for daily and monthly reviews

---

## 3. Audience Covered

Training sessions were provided to:

* Sales leadership
* Commercial and business teams
* Regional and territory managers
* Operations and support teams

Each group was trained based on their **specific reporting and decision requirements**.

---

## 4. Training Materials

The following materials were prepared and shared:

* Dashboard overview documentation
* KPI definitions and calculation logic
* Navigation and usage guidelines
* Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

All materials were shared in a **controlled and non-confidential format**.

---

## 5. Post-Deployment Support

After deployment, ongoing support was provided through:

* Addressing user queries related to data interpretation handled
* Clarifying KPI definitions and filters in case issue in the backend source data 
* Supporting minor enhancements and usability improvements

Feedback received during this phase was tracked and prioritized.

---

## 6. Knowledge Transfer

To ensure continuity and scalability:

* Documentation was maintained for future reference
* Reporting logic and dependencies were explained to relevant teams
* Ownership guidelines were shared for long-term usage

---

## 7. Outcome

The training and support phase resulted in:

* High user adoption and confidence in dashboards
* Reduced dependency on ad-hoc reporting
* Improved consistency in business reviews
* Smooth transition to operational usage
